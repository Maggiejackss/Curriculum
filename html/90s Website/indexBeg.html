The Beginning

The Beginning is necessary and required to connect with and fully manipulate the Neon. This is a difficult and laborious process but you will not be able or allowed to proceed with Guidance from the enlightened Elders.

  1. Proceed: In order to begin you must proceed towards the Neon from within our connectors.
  2. First Step: You must first show you have the ability and willingness to utilize the basic capacity of the Neon. It is a strenuous and seemingly impossible task to begin but with focus and dedication you can complete this task.
  3. Second Step: Next you must make a real world contribution towards the Neon's collective cause. Find any target designated by the Neon of priority level 4 or below.
